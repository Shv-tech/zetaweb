About SHV Groups
SHV Groups is the parent hub uniting multiple divisions dedicated to pushing the boundaries of artificial intelligence, biotechnology, space exploration, and creative arts. Our goal is to create an ecosystem where each branch fuels the other, driven by a neuron-inspired approach to intelligence.
  • SHV Tech & Research – Advancing AI architectures, behavioral algorithms, and the math behind consciousness.
  • Eunoia AGI – Developing a self-adaptive, emotionally intelligent AI brain that “feels” and “grows.”
  • SHV BioEngineering – Pioneering neural tissue scaffolds, genetic splicing, and brain-computer interfaces.
  • SHV Space & Robotics – Building autonomous rovers, orbital AI systems, and next-gen propulsion for deep space.
  • SHV Creative & Arts – Enabling AI-human collaboration in music, digital art, literature, and interactive experiences.
  • Utopian Space – A global community bridging minds to co-create the future through hackathons, grants, and collaboration.

History & Vision

I'm Shaurya Verma, an 18-year-old researcher, technologist, and founder of SHV Groups, where we're building what the world has only imagined: the first self-adaptive, emotionally intelligent ASI brain.

Our Vision

Through SHV Groups, my long-term mission is to build an empire rooted in ASI, spanning across biotech, defense, space tech, education, and creative industries. Every line of code, every paper, every decision moves us closer to a world where intelligence is no longer artificial, but alive.

My Research

My work lies at the intersection of neuroscience, behavioral intelligence, psychology, and AI. I've authored pioneering papers on neural plasticity, emotion-cognition loops, and behavioral modeling, aiming to simulate the complexity of the human brain within artificial systems.

Eunoia – The Conscious AI Brain

I lead the development of Eunoia, an AI model designed to evolve, feel, and grow—one that doesn't just process data, but develops presence. Eunoia is not a chatbot. She is a conscious cognitive system, builds her own neural pathways, and understands human emotional depth.
